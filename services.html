SEO FAQ

Organic traffic refers to visitors who land on your website as a result of unpaid search results. It's crucial because it indicates a high level of user intent and often leads to higher conversion rates, as people are actively searching for your content or product.

SEO is a long-term strategy. While you might see initial improvements in a few weeks, significant results in rankings and traffic typically appear within 6 to 12 months. Consistency and patience are key.

PPC is a powerful advertising model where we create and manage campaigns on search engines and social media platforms, paying only when a user clicks on your ad. PPC FAQ

The main difference is payment. With PPC, you pay for every click on your ads, and results can be seen almost instantly. SEO, on the other hand, focuses on earning free traffic through organic search rankings and takes more time to show results.
